LIÇÃO 14 — There Is / There Are

Nível A1
🎯 Objetivo da Lição

• Usar There is (singular) e There are (plural) para descrever o que existe em um lugar.

• Fazer perguntas com Is there / Are there e negar com There isn't / There aren't.

• Ampliar as preposições de lugar (inside, outside, above, below, around, among, between).

🔥 Warm-Up: O que tem na sua sala?

Olhe ao redor e nomeie 5 coisas que você vê:

1. There is a window.

2. There are two chairs.

3. There is a computer.

4. There are books on the table.

5. There is a lamp next to the door.

💡 "There is" = existe (1 coisa). "There are" = existem (2+ coisas).

🗣️ Diálogo: A Real Estate Agent Shows a House

Agent: Welcome! This is a beautiful house. There is a big living room.

Client: Is there a garden?

Agent: Yes, there is! There is a garden in the backyard. And there are three bedrooms.

Client: Are there two bathrooms?

Agent: No, there aren't. There is only one bathroom.

Client: Is there a garage?

Agent: Yes, there is. The garage is next to the kitchen.

Client: Is there a balcony?

Agent: Yes, there is a nice balcony above the garden. There are flowers all around.

Client: It's lovely! But there isn't a library, is there?

Agent: No, there isn't. But there is space for one!

🔁 Repita com atenção: Pratique o diálogo, prestando atenção ao uso de there is/there are e preposições.

📘 Gramática em Ação: There Is / There Are
1. Afirmativo

There is a kitchen in the house. | There are three bedrooms. | There is a garden outside.

A. Com "some" no plural:

There are some books on the table. | There are some chairs in the living room.

2. Perguntas: Is there / Are there?

Is there a bathroom? | Are there two bedrooms? | Is there a garden behind the house?

B. Short answers:

Is there a garage? → Yes, there is. / No, there isn't. | Are there chairs? → Yes, there are. / No, there aren't.

3. Negativo: There isn't / There aren't

There isn't a library in this house. | There aren't any pillows on the bed. | There isn't a hospital near here.

No negativo com plural, usamos "any": There aren't any books.
📝 Exercícios
A. Complete com There is ou There are.
a kitchen in the house.
two bedrooms upstairs.
a garden in the backyard.
trees around the lake.
a park between the school and the church.
three bathrooms.
B. Escolha a opção correta.
"___ there a balcony?"
"There ___ any chairs in the room."
"There ___ a lake near the city."
"___ there mountains around the town?"
C. Escreva frases sobre sua casa usando There is/are + preposições.

Exemplo: There is a lamp above my desk.

D. Verdadeiro ou Falso?
"There is two bedrooms" — Correto.
"Is there a garden?" — Pergunta correta.
"There aren't any flowers outside" — Negativo correto.
"Among" é usado para indicar posição entre dois itens.
🎭 Prática Oral / Role-Play

Agent: Welcome! There is a big living room.

Client: Is there a garden?

Agent: Yes, there is a garden behind the house.

Client: Are there bedrooms upstairs?

Agent: Yes, there are three bedrooms above the living room.

Client: Is there a garage?

Agent: No, there isn't. But there is space for one.

Client: There are trees around the house, aren't there?

👅 Trava-língua

There are thirty-three thousand trees on that island.

🔁 Repita 3x rápido! Concentre-se no som "th".

🌍 Curiosidade Cultural: Houses in the US vs. Brazil

Nos EUA, a maioria das pessoas vive em casas nos subúrbios, com front yard e backyard, garagem para 2 carros e porão (basement). No Brasil, é mais comum morar em apartamentos nas grandes cidades. No Reino Unido, as terraced houses (casas geminadas) são tradicionais.

💡 Nos EUA: "apartment" e "first floor" = térreo. No Reino Unido: "flat" e "first floor" = segundo andar!
