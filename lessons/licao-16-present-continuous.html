LIÇÃO 16 — Present Continuous

Nível A1
🎯 Objetivo da Lição

• Usar o Present Continuous para falar sobre o que está acontecendo agora: "I am studying."

• Aprender as regras de spelling do -ing (make→making, run→running, study→studying).

• Diferenciar Simple Present × Present Continuous: hábito vs. acontecimento agora.

🔥 Warm-Up: O que você está fazendo agora?

Olhe para si mesmo e para as pessoas ao redor. O que estão fazendo NESTE MOMENTO?

1. I am studying English right now.

2. My mom is cooking dinner.

3. My dog is sleeping on the sofa.

4. It is raining outside.

5. The teacher is talking to the class.

💡 Dica: O Present Continuous é para ações que estão acontecendo AGORA ou ao redor deste momento!

🗣️ Diálogo: Phone Call — What Are You Doing?

Julia: Hi, Mark! What are you doing right now?

Mark: I'm watching TV. What about you?

Julia: I'm studying English. I'm doing my homework.

Mark: Oh, sorry! Am I interrupting?

Julia: No, it's fine! I'm also eating a sandwich. Are you working today?

Mark: No, I'm not working today. I'm relaxing at home.

Julia: Is your sister studying too?

Mark: No, she isn't. She's playing video games. She's always playing!

Julia: Haha! Are your parents working?

Mark: Yes, they are. They're working at the office today. Hey, are you coming to the party tonight?

Julia: Yes, I am! I'm meeting Sarah at 8.

🔁 Repita com atenção: Observe como o Present Continuous descreve ações em andamento!

📘 Gramática em Ação: Present Continuous
1. Afirmativo: am/is/are + verb-ing
PronomeEstruturaExemplo
Iam + verb-ingI am studying. (I'm studying.)
Youare + verb-ingYou are reading. (You're reading.)
He/She/Itis + verb-ingShe is cooking. (She's cooking.)
We/Theyare + verb-ingThey are playing. (They're playing.)
2. Regras de Spelling do -ing

A. Verbo normal: + ing

play → playing | eat → eating | read → reading | study → studying

B. Drop -e: tira o -e e adiciona -ing

make → making | write → writing | dance → dancing | have → having

C. Consoante-vogal-consoante (CVC): dobra a última consoante + -ing

run → running | sit → sitting | swim → swimming | stop → stopping

3. Negativo: am not / isn't / aren't + verb-ing

I'm not sleeping. | She isn't working today. | They aren't studying right now.

4. Perguntas: Am/Is/Are + sujeito + verb-ing?

Are you studying? | Is she cooking? | Are they playing? | Am I interrupting?

5. Simple Present × Present Continuous
Simple Present (hábito)Present Continuous (agora)
I study English every day.I am studying English right now.
She works in a hospital.She is working from home today.
They play soccer on Fridays.They are playing soccer at the moment.
Dica: Simple Present = rotina/hábito (always, usually, every day). Present Continuous = agora (now, right now, at the moment).

D. Verdadeiro ou Falso?
"I am makeing dinner" — Spelling correto.
"She is runing" — Spelling correto.
"Are you studying?" — Pergunta correta no Present Continuous.
"I study English right now" — Frase correta para algo acontecendo agora.

🌍 Curiosidade Cultural: Present Continuous for Future

Em inglês, usamos o Present Continuous para planos futuros confirmados! "I'm meeting John tomorrow" significa "Vou me encontrar com o John amanhã" — é um plano, não algo acontecendo agora. É muito comum em conversas!

Exemplos: "I'm flying to London next week." | "We're having dinner at 7 tonight." | "She's starting a new job on Monday."

💡 Dica: Quando alguém pergunta "What are you doing tonight?", geralmente quer saber seus planos, não o que você está fazendo neste segundo!
